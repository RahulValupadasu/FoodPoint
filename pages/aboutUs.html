Who are we

Foodpoint is a technology company that connects people with the best in their cities. We do this by empowering local businesses and in turn, generate new ways for people to earn, work and live. We started by facilitating door-to-door delivery, but we see this as just the beginning of connecting people with possibility — easier evenings, happier days, bigger savings accounts, wider nets and stronger communities.Food Point began operations by signing up a few restaurants in Canada. Soon enough, the first team of Hunger Saviors came into action to deliver food within 40 minutes. Shortly after, Food Point raised its first round of funding and launched the app in April of 2020.

Fun fact: The technology that delivers great food right to your doorstep was completely developed in-house!

With love and support from consumers, Food Point expanded far and wide, first through the entire city of Toranto and then across the entire country. As they say, the rest is history. To ensure and focus on better consumer experience, Food Point has launched a platform for partners, wherein Food Point partners can order premium packaging material at a reasonable cost and help us in providing an awesome experience for the consumers.

Food Point has a good team of Chef's, who are expertise in cooking wide varieties of special dishes of different countries. There by the customers don't miss their native food as well as enjoys the taste of other special dishes of different countries. Customers also have the advantage of tracking their order and can order to any location. They can even get the referral bonous and coupons or free delivery options based on their usage.

We add value to people's lives through seamless online food delivery and pickup.

At Foodpoint, we know you deserve great delivery. Our world-class, unparalleled technology powers millions of orders every month to bring hungry Canadians the food they want, delivered. Foodpoint is Canada's leading and largest food delivery network, with tens of thousands of restaurants coast-to-coast in hundreds of communities across Canada
